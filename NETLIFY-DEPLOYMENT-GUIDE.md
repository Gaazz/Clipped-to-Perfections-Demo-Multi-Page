## üìã DEPLOYMENT STEPS (5 MINUTES):

### STEP 1: Sign Up for Netlify
1. Go to https://www.netlify.com
2. Click "Sign up" (top right)
3. Choose "Sign up with email" or use GitHub/GitLab
4. Verify your email

### STEP 2: Deploy Your Site
1. Log into Netlify
2. Click **"Add new site"** ‚Üí **"Deploy manually"**
3. **Drag and drop** the ZIP file (CLIPPED-NETLIFY-READY.zip)
4. Wait 30 seconds - site is now LIVE!
5. You'll get a URL like: `random-name-12345.netlify.app`

### STEP 3: Set Up Form Notifications
1. In Netlify dashboard, click on your site
2. Go to **"Forms"** in the left menu
3. You'll see your "contact" form listed
4. Click **"Form notifications"**
5. Click **"Add notification"** ‚Üí **"Email notification"**
6. Enter client's email: `clippedtoperfection@gmail.com`
7. Choose "New form submission"
8. Click **"Save"**

‚úÖ **DONE!** Forms will now email the client directly!
